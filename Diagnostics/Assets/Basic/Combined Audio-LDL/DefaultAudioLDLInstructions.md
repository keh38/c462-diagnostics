- Now we want to measure a couple of things.
- We want to find the softest sounds that you can hear
- then we want you to judge the **loudness** of those sounds
[br]
- There are several sliders on the following screen
- When prompted, drag the slider to the right until you can just barely hear a sound
- Go back and forth a couple times just to be sure you get the right spot
- Then press the button to lock in your choice
[br]
- Then continue dragging the slider to the right to make the sound louder and louder
- Stop when the sound is as loud as you can tolerate
- Go back and forth a couple times just to be sure you get the right spot
- Then press the button to lock in your choice
[br]
- You will adjust many sliders on this test
- The pitch and ear will vary from slider to slider
- So you will probably adjust each to a different point. That's OK
[br]
- The levels of the remaining tests will be set based on your loudness judgments during this test.
- For the remaining tests, we will never present a sound that meets or exceeds the level that you said was uncomfortable.
